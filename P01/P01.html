Bradning Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

This style guide is for an imaginary company that sells a wide variety of organic tea and tea accessories. The Company aims to offer high-quality tea leaf and unique flavors. On this website, customers will be able to know more about the ingredients, and pick their favorite blended tea from popular recipes or customize their own blend. The company also offers hand-crafted tea drink in local stores.

Who is the company to potential customers?

Majority part of our potential customers will be tea lovers who are looking for a pleasant, relaxing place in which to indulge their love of tea, share it with friends, and learn more about tea varieties, cultivation, and health properties.

Other potential customers may be gift buyers, tourists, socializer and people who intakes tea as health food.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Nowadays, tea is growing in popularity and more people are drinking tea in the mornings as a healthier substitute for coffee. The company provides the local upper-middle-class community with a wide variety of selction, elegant decorative tea accessories and unique tea for gifts. Those customers will be glad to pay the price premium for our products in exchange for the high quality, great taste, and sense of prestige they receive.
