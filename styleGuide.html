What does the company do, or offer?

TeaLife is a platform that sells a variety of organic tea and tea accessories. The Company aims to offer high-quality yet affordable tea leaves with unique flavors.

-Customers will be offered with abundance of information regarding origin of tea leaves, its ingredients, and pick their favorite from popular recipes or customize their own blend.

The company also offers hand-crafted tea drink in local stores. Who is the company to potential customers?

- Our targeted audience will be tea lovers especially the younger generation who are looking for unique, relaxing place in which to indulge their love of tea and be able to share it with friends and make at home.

- Other potential customers may be gift buyers, tourists, socializer and people who intakes tea as health food.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

- As tea is growing in popularity and more people are seeking a healthier lifestyle, we believe a simple yet vibrant styling like website will be suitable for tea lovers who are looking to purchase or know more about tea.
